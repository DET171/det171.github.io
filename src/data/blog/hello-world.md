Welcome to my blog! 🎉

This is a sample post to help you get started with your new blog. Feel free to edit or delete this post and create your own content.

## What's next?

Here are some things you can do to customize your blog:

1. **Update site configuration** - Edit `src/config.ts` to customize your site title, description, and other settings
2. **Create new posts** - Add `.md` or `.mdx` files to `src/data/blog/`
3. **Add galleries** - Create image galleries in `src/data/galleries/` (optional)
4. **Customize styling** - Modify the theme and styles to match your preferences
5. **Deploy** - Build and deploy your blog to your favorite hosting platform

## Writing Posts

Posts support full Markdown syntax plus additional features:

- **Code blocks** with syntax highlighting
- **Tables** for organizing data
- **Images** and media embeds
- **MDX components** for interactive content

Happy blogging! ✨
